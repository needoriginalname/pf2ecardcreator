# Privacy Policy

## Overview

NeedOriginalPf2eTools is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

**Minimal Data Collection**

NeedOriginalPf2eTools operates primarily in your browser. We collect minimal data:

- **Usage Analytics** (optional) - If you have analytics enabled, we may collect anonymized usage data to improve the application
- **Browser Cache** - Your character data is stored locally in your browser

### What We Don't Collect

- We do NOT require account creation or login
- We do NOT store your personal information on our servers
- We do NOT track your identity
- We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties
- We do NOT store character data on our servers (unless you explicitly choose to export/share)

## Local Storage

**Your Character Data**

Character data you create is stored locally in your browser using browser storage mechanisms. This means:

- Your data stays on your device
- Your data is not transmitted to our servers
- Clearing your browser data will clear your saved characters
- You can export your characters for backup purposes

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies, primarily for:
- Site preferences (theme, language, etc.)
- Session management

Third-party cookies are limited and only used for essential services.

## Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to external websites and services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies independently.

## Security

While we strive to protect your information, no security system is 100% secure. The best security for your character data is using the local storage feature and not sharing sensitive information.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through:
- Updates to this document
- Announcements on the website
- GitHub repository updates

The date of the last update will be displayed at the bottom of this page.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please:

- Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/needoriginalname/pf2ecardcreator)
- Review our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/needoriginalname/pf2ecardcreator) for contact information

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access any data we have about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of analytics collection
- Data portability (export your characters)
